Welcome to Hair by Dioni Nicole, where beauty and expertise blend seamlessly. I am Jessica Barnes, a hairstylist with over two decades of experience. Our primary mission is to create healthy, up-to-date styles for all our clients, offering instant transformations that are easy to maintain at home. Inspired by my daughter and my passion for creating beauty products and providing beautiful hair services, we are dedicated to restoring the health of your hair. My specialties are precision haircuts, advanced hair loss services, healthy weaving techniques, and crafting custom wigs. I am an expert in working with all hair textures and serving clients of all races. To ensure we provide the best, I continually update my skills by attending classes every year. I am also dedicated to sharing knowledge as an educator and traveling stylist, teaching precision cuts and custom wigs to other salons. At Hair by Dioni Nicole, our commitment to beauty and health is unwavering. Join us for a transformational experience where the health and beauty of your hair are our top priority!

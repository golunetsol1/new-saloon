Totally Together Beauty Studio is your place for natural and relaxed hair care. Sederia Thomas, a native Houstonian, has been a licensed stylist since 2003 and welcomes you to her private salon suite. Sederia has extensive knowledge of relaxed and natural hair which enables her to know the best care and products for your individual hair needs. She prefers to use products such as Design Essentials, Moroccan Oil, E'tae, and Hair Rules but has access to other products as well if you have any special needs. Professional color lines such as Schwarzkopf, Paul Mitchell, and Goldwell are used mostly. The health of your hair regardless of whether it is relaxed or natural is her main focus. You will find that her extensive knowledge of the curly and relaxed hair will give you an experience you might not find elsewhere. You will find at Totally Together Beauty Studio that Sederia will have a clean, fun, and relaxed atmosphere.

It has been said that Sederia has a fun and bubbly personality that will keep you laughing during your visit! She knows your time is valuable and will respect your time by not overbooking. Please text her to inquire about a complimentary consultation.

Payments Accepted: Cash, CashApp, Apple Pay, PayPal, Visa, MasterCard, ATM on-site.

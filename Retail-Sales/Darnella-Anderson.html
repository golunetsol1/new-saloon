Hello, thanks for taking the time to read a little about me. My name is Nella and I was born and raised in Houston, TX, and am very familiar with the area. I have always been interested in doing hair since I was a little girl who grew up watching my grandmother, Leona, in her own salon. I received my cosmetology degree from San Jacinto College South and pursued additional salon industry education outside of college to perfect my skills. Additionally, I acquired training in full body waxing and have added that to my services. I am very passionate and knowledgeable about hair and body waxing as a result of my drive. The things I love to do are color; all types of color from highlighting to coloring weave to coloring natural hair. I love weaving and extensions, natural hair, wig making, and full body waxing. I pay close attention to details and respect everyone's time by not overbooking. I work with all ethnicities and everyone is welcome at Da Image Addict. I take pride in my craft and every client who is in my presence.

I'm taking all necessary precautions when it comes to COVID-19 suite 602.

I accept cash and most major credit cards. ATM is onsite. Credit Cards will be charged an additional 3%.

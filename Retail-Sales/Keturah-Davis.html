Hey there! I am Keturah Davis, the Owner and Operator of The Healthy Hair Retreat for "Beauty and Wellness". Our mission is to help women focus on SELF MASTERY through Mind, Body, and Beauty. Helping you to Love the Life you Desire and Deserve! The Health of your hair and body is our HIGHEST PRIORITY. I am a licensed cosmetologist with 27 years of experience, servicing a multicultural clientele. I am also a certified Body Sculpting and Cavitation Specialist. I take pride in using the BEST products available. I am dedicated to service and sincerely enjoy bringing smiles to others who are happy with their results, bringing them a step closer to self-love and self-mastery.
